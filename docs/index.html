Hands & pack

Backspaceclose any menu
Escpause menu (volume, keys)

The situation

The Meridian Survey Company owns your passage debt: 2000 credits. Survey islands, open what you find, come back alive. They garnish 60% of every payout — what you haul is yours, to sell at their store, at their prices.

Eat before the hunger bar fills. Mind the thickets — what you can't see through, you can hide in. So can everything else. Retrieval is billable.

Work the debt to zero and you're free.
